Coordinator Schedule

Task Management

This use case involves the coordination and management of the coordinator's tasks and schedule within the system.

Precondition

The coordinator must have valid credentials and the appropriate role permissions for schedule management.

Trigger

The use case is initialized when the coordinator selects the 'Schedule Tasks' functionality.
